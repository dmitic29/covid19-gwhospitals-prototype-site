Preventing the Spread of COVID-19

Step 1

Wash Your Hands

Wash your hands frequently.

Studies show you are 50% less likely to get sick if you wash your hands ferquently. Especially when you were previously out in public.

Step 2

Stay At Home If You Feel Unwell

If you think you might be sick, don't put others at risk.

Social distancing can be dificult but it is necessary in order for us to be able to control the spread of the virus.

Step 3

Cover Your Mouth When You Cough/Sneeze

Coronavirus is spread from person to person in this way mainly.

You will significantly reduce the risk of spreading the disease if you make sure your coughs and sneezes are covered by a napkin or a sleeve as they occur.

Step 4

Avoid Unnecessary Gatherings

If you do not have to meet and have contact with people don't do it.

This is your biggest risk factor, and eliminating it will drastically reduce your odds of catching COVID-19.
